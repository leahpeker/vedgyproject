We use cookies to provide essential functionality for our service. Here's what you need to know:

What Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies (Essential): Required for you to log in and stay logged in while using VedgyProject. These expire when you close your browser or after 7 days.
  • CSRF Protection Cookies (Essential): Security cookies that protect your account from unauthorized actions. These are strictly necessary for the security of our service.

Why We Use Cookies

All cookies we use are strictly necessary for the website to function properly. They enable:

  • User authentication (keeping you logged in)
  • Security protection against cross-site attacks
  • Maintaining your session as you navigate the site

Managing Cookies

Because our cookies are essential for the website to work, disabling them will prevent you from logging in and using core features of VedgyProject. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but please note that blocking essential cookies will limit your ability to use our service.

We do not use:

  • Analytics or tracking cookies

  • Third-party cookies (except those strictly necessary for payment processing)
